Hello! Thank you for visiting my website! I am Computer Science Masters student at the Kalhert School of Computing, College of Engineering, Univeristy of Utah, and a Graduate Research Assistant at the Scientific Computing and Imaging (SCI) Institute, where I am advised by Prof. Shireen Elhabian . Before that,I earned my BE in Computer Engineering from the University of Mumbai.

My Masters research is at the intersection of 3D Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with an emphasis on Registration and Deep Learning. My current projects involve estimating and quantifying uncertainty in automated, deep learning based, 3D medical image segmentation models.

In my leisure time, I find joy in exploring the scenic trails of Utah through hiking. I also have a passion for swimming, which offers a refreshing counterbalance to my outdoor adventures. Baking allows me to express my creativity in the kitchen, while painting serves as my canvas for artistic expression. Dancing, on the other hand, keeps me energized and in tune with the rhythm of life. Each of these activities enriches my life in unique ways,while inspiring creativity and balance in my professional endeavors.
